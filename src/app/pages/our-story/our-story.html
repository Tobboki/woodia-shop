Furniture used to be personal

It carried the mark of the craftsman — the subtle curve of a hand-finished edge, the precision of a joint cut with care, the pride of someone who built it to last.

Today, much of the furniture industry is controlled by large galleries and showrooms. Mass production replaced craftsmanship. Customers choose from what’s available — not from what they truly want. Meanwhile, thousands of skilled carpenters remain unseen, competing for limited exposure rather than for their actual talent.

We believe there is a better way!

The Problem We Saw

  • Customers are forced to buy what galleries display.

  • Custom furniture is expensive, slow, and often inaccessible.

  • Skilled carpenters struggle to find consistent, fair opportunities.

  • Talent exists — but visibility does not.

The system favors middlemen, not makers.

Our Idea

We built a platform that brings craftsmanship back to the center.Instead of browsing finished products, customers start with an idea.

They can

  • Select a base furniture piece

  • Customize dimensions, materials, finishes, and details

  • Post their project to a community of verified carpenters

From there, professional craftsmen compete by submitting

  • Their proposed price

  • Estimated completion time

  • Portfolio samples

  • Customer ratings

The result?

  • Competitive pricing.

  • Transparent timelines

  • True customization.

  • Direct connection between maker and customer.
